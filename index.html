What are Akan names

Akan names are used in Ghanian culture and are based on gender and date of birth. The children's names are asssigned once the gender is known as well as the day of the week they are born. This is refered to as a "Day Name."

Male

Sunday: Kwasi
Monday: Kwadwo
Tuesday: Kwabena
Wednesday: Kwaku
Thursday: Yaw
Friday: Kofi
Saturday: Kwame

Female

Sunday: Akosua
Monday: Adwoa
Tuesday: Abenaa
Wednesday: Akua
Thursday: Yaa
Friday: Afua
Saturday: Ama
